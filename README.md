# fox-talks

A Duolingo-style app designed for individuals with speech difficulties to practice speaking and pronunciation, serving as an additional aid for speech therapy and encouraging consistent practice.

**Targets**: Individuals with speech impairments. children, Adults experiencing speech problems due to the lack of proper therapy during their childhood, people discourgaed from regular speech therapy, due to financial issues. individuals who lose speech abilities after a medical event

**What it Solves**: 
Individuals with speech challenges can build greater confidence through regular, consis- tent practice instead of relying only on costly speech therapy sessions. It also offers a judgment-free space for shy users.

**How it works**: 
The concept is now at the MVP phase; users will practice speaking inside the app while engaging in mini-games offering bite-sized learning like Duolingo. They can record their voice alongside sample audio. Pronunciation will be assessed by AI, and users earn badges for effort, gradually guiding them to perfect pronunciation. Users can select their speech goals upfront.
